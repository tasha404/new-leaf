ACTIVITIES

SUPPORTING ANIMAL SHELTERS:
One impactful way to contribute is by supporting animal shelters in regions struck by environmental disasters. These shelters provide crucial care for displaced animals alongside human populations. Consider volunteering your time or donating resources to help these shelters offer the necessary medical attention and shelter to animals in need.
TRAINING FOR ANIMAL RESCUE OPERATIONS:
Empowering ourselves with the skills needed to assist in animal rescue operations during emergencies can make a real impact. Consider attending training programs that equip individuals with the knowledge and abilities required to support animals in crisis situations.
